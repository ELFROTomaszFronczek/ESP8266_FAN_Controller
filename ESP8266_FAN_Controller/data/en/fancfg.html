Minimum speed - the minimum value of rotational speed in percent. This prevents the fan from stopping. Lower speeds will be increased to this value.
 
Boost - accelerates the fan to 50 percent for 2 seconds, enabling start at very slow speeds.

Device Status:
- blinking about 1 sec - connecting to WiFi.
- short flashes long pause - normal operation, WiFi connected
- very short flashes and a short break - work in Access Point mode
PWM frequency:
The PWM frequency determines the basic motor control frequency. Value in the range 100Hz 40000Hz.
For ESP8266, the default frequency is 1000Hz. However, depending on the motor used, PWM frequencies of 20-20000 Hz may be audible during operation. (Fan may beep).
22000Hz recommended.
